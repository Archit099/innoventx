Why Should you Enroll in this course?

The current industry i.e industry 4.0 is completely based on robotics, automation & IoT. Robotics is a filed where developments and inventions cannot be made on paper. You will have to go through a lot of failures, but this course will teach you never to give up on anything till its done.

The most required skill for this generation to survive in market is the skill of continuous learning. This course will help you kick start to develop this skill of continuous learning.

How This course would help you develop the skill of continuous learing?

After the two days workshop a TBT(Task Based Training will be conducted for all the participants.

TBT is a rigorous training program where the participants will be trained on robotics for 1 month, where they will be given task every week to solve.

They will have to solve this task on their own, but, if participants gets stuck anywhere in between ,our team is always their to help them & complete the task.

This approach will help the students to learn new concepts on their own, to solve the task given.

At the end this will help them kick start their continuous learning skill.

All participants will receive the certificate of completion at the end of this program.

What will you gain after this workshop?

You will have an edge over your fellow classmates and friends who did not attend the workshop. You will be knowing how to make you own robot and how the sensor work in real environment, you will know how to calibrate sensors according to your environment. You will learn how to code in arduino IDE ,a software ,that is widely used in the robotics and electronics industries.

The most important skill you are going to learn is how to never give up, and work until the work is done.
